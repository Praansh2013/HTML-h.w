Bengali cuisine

Bengali cuisine is known for its subtle yet complex flavours, with a perfect balance of sweet, sour, bitter, and spicy tastes. The use of mustard oil, panch phoron (a blend of five whole spices), and the fragrant spice blend garam masala impart a distinct aroma and taste to the dishes.


Bihari cuisine

The cuisine of Bihar is very wholesome. Dal and bhat is the staple food of Bihar like many other parts of India. And mustard oil along with spices like cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, nigella seeds, fennel seeds, carom seeds and red chillies are commonly used in cooking.

Kashmiri cuisine

Kashmiri cuisine is a rich and flavorful cuisine that reflects the region's history and cultural exchanges. It's known for its meat dishes, and is influenced by Persian, Central Asian, and Indian culinary traditions.
